Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026

Mantra is a personal intelligence system. This policy explains what information we collect, why we use it, when we share it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

We collect information you choose to provide, including account details, messages, files, goals, notes, contacts, preferences, and other content you add to Mantra.

Connected services

If you connect services such as email, calendar, contacts, financial accounts, health services, or social platforms, we receive the information needed to provide the features you request. The information available to Mantra depends on the permissions you grant.

Usage and device information

We collect limited technical information about how you access and use Mantra, such as browser or device type, IP address, timestamps, feature usage, diagnostics, and crash data.

2. How we use information

We use information to:

  • provide, personalize, maintain, and improve Mantra;
  • perform actions and generate responses you request;
  • connect and synchronize services you authorize;
  • protect the security, integrity, and reliability of Mantra;
  • communicate with you about the service; and
  • comply with law and enforce our agreements.

3. Artificial intelligence and model providers

Mantra uses artificial intelligence providers to process requests and generate results. We send those providers only the information reasonably needed to fulfill a request, subject to their applicable data-processing terms and our contractual controls. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your private content to train public or shared AI models without your explicit consent.

4. How we share information

We may share information with service providers that help us operate Mantra, including hosting, storage, authentication, analytics, communications, payment, and AI infrastructure providers. We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect rights or safety, in connection with a corporate transaction, or when you direct us to share it. We do not sell personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

5. Data retention and deletion

We retain information for as long as needed to provide Mantra, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and protect the service. You may request deletion of your account and personal information. Some information may remain temporarily in backups or be retained when legally required.

6. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your choices and rights

You may update account information, disconnect integrated services, change permissions through the relevant provider, or request access, correction, export, or deletion of personal information. Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights and the right to appeal a decision about your request.

8. Children

Mantra is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can delete it.

9. International data transfers

We and our providers may process information in the United States and other countries. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as Mantra evolves. We will post the revised policy with a new effective date and provide additional notice when required by law.

11. Contact us

For privacy questions or requests, email privacy@trymantra.ai.
