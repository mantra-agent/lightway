Frequently asked questions

Is this just another AI chatbot?

No. Mantra is a system with memory that compounds over time, understands your goals and relationships, and works proactively alongside you.

What happens to my data?

Your data is yours. Mantra doesn't train on it, sell it, or share it. You can export or delete everything at any time.

How is this different from Notion / Obsidian / Apple Notes?

Those are capture tools. Mantra is what happens after capture. It connects notes to goals, goals to relationships, and relationships to your calendar.

Do I need to spend a lot of time setting it up?

No. Start with a few goals and priorities. Mantra starts immediately and gets smarter over time.
