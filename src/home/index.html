Frequently asked questions

Is this just another AI chatbot?

No. Mantra is a system, not a chat window. It has memory that compounds over time, understands your goals and relationships, and works proactively alongside you. You can talk to it, but that's one of many ways to interact.

What happens to my data?

Your data is yours. Mantra doesn't train on your information, sell it, or share it. You can export or delete everything at any time. Privacy isn't a feature. It's the foundation.

How is this different from Notion / Obsidian / Apple Notes?

Those are excellent capture tools. Mantra is what happens after you capture. It connects your notes to your goals, your goals to your relationships, and your relationships to your calendar. It doesn't replace your notes app. It makes your notes useful.

Do I need to spend a lot of time setting it up?

No. Start with a few goals and priorities. Mantra starts working immediately and gets smarter over time. There's no "right way" to use it, and you don't need to reorganize your life to get value.
